Our Mission

Alimaya Foundation is a registered NGO in Ghana dedicated to fighting poverty and creating opportunities for growth. We focus on education, healthcare, and community empowerment.

Our vision is to ensure every child has access to learning, every family has access to healthcare, and every community benefits from clean water, food banks, and sustainable livelihoods.

We are in the early stages of our journey, but with your support, these goals will become a reality.

Our Projects

We are preparing to launch impactful projects in education, healthcare, and community empowerment.

Education Support

Our goal is to provide textbooks, school supplies, and scholarships to children in underserved communities. We are also exploring solar-powered study lamps for evening learning.

Healthcare Outreach

We plan to organize free medical screenings, distribute mosquito nets, and provide rural clinics with essential supplies to improve access to healthcare.

Community Empowerment

We are committed to empowering women and youth through vocational training in tailoring, bead-making, and farming — creating pathways to sustainable income.
